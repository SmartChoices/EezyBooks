Program Licensing

As noted, you must purchase a license within 30 days of installation.  Failure to do so will disable the most of the functions.

To register, click the Help -> Purchase License menu item.  You will be taken to the web site where you can purchase a license using PayPal.  On the website, you will see this page:

Notice the highlighted fields... you will put the MAC address in the field for the program you are going to purchase the license for.  (only one occurrance of the MAC address is necessary).  You get the MAC address from either the Book or Media Inventory program, as shown here:

Click on Help, then on About... you will see the following screen, where the MAC address is highlighted.  Click on the Copy button, which will copy the MAC address to the clipboard.

 

We will respond with an email containing your License code, which you should copy and paste into the License Screen above.  Click Enter when completed and your information will be saved.
