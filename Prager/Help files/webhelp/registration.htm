Program Licensing

As noted, you must purchase a license within 30 days of installation.  Failure to do so will disable the most of the functions.

To register, click the Help -> Purchase License menu item.  You will be taken to the web site where you can purchase a license using PayPal. 

We will respond with an email containing your Unlock code, which you should copy and paste into the License Screen above.  Click Enter when completed and your information will be saved.
