Using Auto Price Lookup

Checking Options

On the Program Options page, there is a menu check-box for Automatic Pricing Lookup.  When this option is checked, it works two ways:  when you enter the ISBN and click Get Info w/ ISBN, after the detail has been obtained from the internet, it will automatically go and check prices, displaying what it found on the screen below.  Other way it works is if you just want to check prices on a selected book (select a book by clicking on the book in the Database Panel above the tabs) and click Get Prices from Internet.  This will do the same as described above, only you can do it at any time after the book has been entered.  The results are shown below.  At the present time, this only works for books with ISBNs (not ASINs).

 

NOTE:  It is to your advantage to notify us when this is not working.  We get the prices from several websites that may change how they display the prices.  When they change, we are not able to get the prices, therefore we need to be notified.  Please open a ticket at our Bug Tracking page.
